<h2 style="font-weight:700;font-size:31px;line-height:1.15;color:#1b1b1d;letter-spacing:-.022em;margin:0 0 .5em;">What is the RPR Reports Widget?</h2>
<p style="font-size:18px;line-height:1.75;color:#3d3d41;margin:0 0 1.1em;">The RPR Reports Widget is an embeddable tool that lets REALTORS® offer selected RPR reports directly from their own website or landing page.</p>
<p style="font-size:18px;line-height:1.75;color:#3d3d41;margin:0 0 1.1em;">You choose the reports. Your visitor chooses the area they care about. The widget collects their contact information, sends the lead where you want it to go and then gives the visitor access to the matching RPR report.</p>
<p style="font-size:22px;line-height:1.5;font-weight:300;letter-spacing:-.01em;color:#1b1b1d;margin:0;">RPR provides the report and the local data. You bring the interpretation.</p>

<p style="font-style:italic;font-weight:300;color:#77736c;font-size:21px;margin:0 0 1.1em;padding-left:18px;border-left:2px solid #e4e8ee;">“What information would someone visiting this page actually want?”</p>
<p style="font-size:18px;line-height:1.75;color:#3d3d41;margin:0 0 1.1em;">Once you know the answer, you can build or customize an RPR report around that need, add it to the widget and create a stronger reason for someone to engage.</p>

<p style="font-size:22px;line-height:1.5;font-weight:300;letter-spacing:-.01em;color:#1b1b1d;margin:0;">The more intentionally you configure the report, the more useful the experience becomes for both the visitor and your follow-up.</p>
